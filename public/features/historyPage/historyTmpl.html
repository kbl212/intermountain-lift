History 1969-2016

Ray and Valora Crandall founded Intermountain Lift in Springville, Utah, April 29, 1969. Springville is located in the central western United States and is strategically based to ship products anywhere nationally or internationally. The company was initially started to supply some of the local ski areas and a small tramway with major rebuilding, repair, and parts service. Later the company ventured into more technical removal, rebuilding, and installation projects for various ski lifts, and rope tows.

The size of the facility and personnel gradually increased to where the company was able to begin serving other markets in the Amusement, Transportation, Industrial And Commercial industries with their specialized steel fabrication requirements.
